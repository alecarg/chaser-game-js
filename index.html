<DEV BATTLE>

You will impersonate a player in a game.
Your objective is to escape from the chaser, an AI.
Other players will be trying to escape too... so be smart!
Last person standing wins :)

You control your character by writing javascript.
The game develops turn by turn.
Each turn, one player moves by having it's code execute.
The chaser also gets its own turn.

We will give you methods you can use to understand the context of every turn.
For example, you will be able to know where the chaser is in relation to your position.
These will be available for you to consult at all times.

The rest will be mostly writing javascript.
The suggestion is to think about the strategy first, and then try to implement it
Functions are your friend!

You will have 10 minutes to complete your code.
Google is accessible as a tab at the top to check javascript syntax, etc.
